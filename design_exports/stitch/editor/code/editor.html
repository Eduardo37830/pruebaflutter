The Quiet Hour

The dawn always brings a certain stillness. It is a quiet that is not merely the absence of sound, but a tangible presence. The kind of presence that settles over the desk, pulling the scattered thoughts of yesterday into a single, focused point.

I looked at the blank page, noting how the slight texture of the paper seemed to catch the early light. There is an intimidation in starting, a fear of breaking the silence with the wrong words. Yet, the ink demands to be used.
